https://selfonashelf.vercel.app/


### features 
-  each element of the shelf features a relevant popup to its icon.
-  The pigeon will give you alot of wisdom , and it might get a bit melodic.
-  The book can be navigated with arrows , same for the camera rolls and cat pics , and the poem umm well it can be _scrolled_.
-  The omnitrix is my personal favourite , you can hover on it to activate the core and click it to transform your cursor.
-  The "Hourglass" , "dino" and "whiteboard" icons display iframes to public sites , while the "Book" , "poem", "camera" , "gmail" and "catpaw" icon to the ones i made.
-  The socials are all links except for the Gmail icon which is a window for Emailjs.
-  The minecraft icon will send you to minecraft classic on a web browser if you click on the "play minecraft?" button.
-  The whiteboard opens up a whiteboard through tldraw which can be edited by anyone (yes its kinda chaotic i guess)
-  The dino lets you play the most interesting dino game i ever played.
-  The sound effectsssssss , they are amusing.
-  also each icon on the shelf displays a description/tip on hover.
